About Poonkavanam


Poonkavanam is not just a house—it is a home built on friendship, respect, laughter, and unforgettable memories. Nine students from different backgrounds live together like one family, supporting each other through every challenge and celebrating every success.


Every meal shared, every late-night conversation, every exam season, and every celebration has strengthened the bond between us. Poonkavanam will always remain a place filled with happiness, unity, and memories that last a lifetime.
